Tacos de Carne Asada

Description

Tacos de Carne Asada are a popular Mexican dish featuring marinated grilled steak served in soft tortillas. The steak is typically marinated in lime juice, garlic, and a blend of spices before being grilled to perfection. These tacos are often topped with fresh cilantro, onions, and a squeeze of lime for extra flavor.

Ingredients

For the Carne Asada

  • Flank steak or skirt steak
  • Lime juice
  • Orange juice
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Olive oil

For the Tacos

  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Onions (diced)
  • Cilantro (chopped)
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa, avocado, or hot sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Marinade

    2. Mix lime juice, orange juice, garlic, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, and olive oil. Marinate steak for at least 1 hour (preferably overnight).

  2. Grill the Meat

    3. Heat a grill or pan over medium-high heat. Grill the marinated steak for about 4-5 minutes per side, depending on thickness. Let rest before slicing thinly against the grain.

  3. Warm the Tortillas

    4. Lightly heat corn or flour tortillas on a skillet until soft and warm.

  4. Assemble the Tacos

    5. Place the grilled carne asada on the tortillas. Top with diced onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.

  5. Serve

    6. Serve with your favorite toppings such as salsa, avocado, or hot sauce. Pair with rice or beans for a complete meal.
