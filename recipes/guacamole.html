Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a classic Mexican dip made from mashed avocados, lime, and other fresh ingredients. It’s typically served with tortilla chips, tacos, or as a side to other Mexican dishes. You can customize it with ingredients like cilantro, tomatoes, and jalapeños for added flavor and heat.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1/4 cup red onion
  • 1-2 tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lime (juice)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapeño

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients

    2. In a bowl, mash 3 ripe avocados. Add 1/4 cup diced red onion, chopped tomatoes, and minced garlic.

  2. Season

    3. Squeeze in the juice of 1 lime, then season with salt and pepper to taste. Optionally, add chopped cilantro and diced jalapeño for extra flavor.

  3. Mix and Taste

    4. Mix everything until well combined. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.

  4. Serve

    5. Serve immediately with tortilla chips, tacos, or as a side to any Mexican dish. Garnish with extra cilantro or lime wedges.
